Features

Guess Who Bot is a fun and interactive Discord bot that allows users to play the classic Guess Who game with friends.

  • Start and manage Guess Who games
  • Ask questions about the mystery character
  • Make guesses and win games
  • Track your profile with XP and wins
  • View the leaderboard
  • Get help and learn how to play

Commands

For a full list of commands and their usage, visit the Documentation page.

Usage Instructions

Invite the bot to your server and use the slash commands to interact with it. Start a game with /startgame, ask questions with /ask, and make guesses with /guess.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. The bot only collects and stores necessary information to manage games and user profiles. No personal data is shared with third parties.

Bot Verification

To get the bot verified, ensure you have filled out all required fields in the Discord Developer Portal, and provide this website as part of your verification application to showcase the bot's features, commands, and privacy policy.
